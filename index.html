Our Story:

In 2020, Michael and Steve felt the urgency to connect with the music, the nature, and the streets of Dublin. They started to play in different areas of the city center. When they played, no matter what string instrument they brought with them, someone always stopped and asked for information regarding how to learn guitar or every other string instrument. In other occasion, they started to play with them. “The Sound of the Strings” Street Music Academy was born from a spontaneous desire for aggregation, and a passion for music and string instruments. No buildings or closed spaces; Dublin parks, and streets will be your classrooms. The courses are free of charge and intended to make music accessible to everyone. You only have to take your instrument, your smile, join the class, and have fun.
